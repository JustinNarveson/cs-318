Crispex Toffee Treat

Ingredients

  • 12 oz. box of Crispex
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 1/2 cup of dark Karo syrup
  • 2 cups chopped pecans
  • 1 cup brown sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 250 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Bring the butter, brown suger and dark karo syrup to a boil.
  3. In a 9x13 pan mix cereal and pecans.
  4. Pour sugar mixture over cereal in pan and mix.
  5. Bake at 250 for an hour stirring every 15 mins.
  6. Pour on wax paper to cool.
  7. Once cool break it up and store in air tight containers.
