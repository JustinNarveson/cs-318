Why the Packers?

The Packers are the most storied franchise in the NFL and hae been a successful small market team for a long time. I grew in southeastern Wisconsin and have been a lifelong Packers fan. I even own part of the team as they are the only team in the NFL that allows fans to buy shares of the team.

Super Bowls

  1. 9 World Championships
  2. 1966 vs. Kansas City Chiefs
  3. 1967 vs. Oakland Raiders
  4. 1996 vs. New England Patriots
  5. 2010 vs. Pittsburgh Steelers

The Packers have the most world championships if you are including the NFL-AFL merger. This makes them one of most successful franchises of all time and doing so in what is considered a small market of Green Bay. Green Bay is town of only 100 thousand which is impressive to have this much athletic success in such a small town compared to other cities with franchises.
